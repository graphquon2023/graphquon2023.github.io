About

GraphQUON 2023 is the annual pre-SIGGRAPH workshop, organized by Central-Canadian computer graphics labs in Quebec and Ontario. It is an opportunity for students to present their prospective SIGGRAPH submissions to a friendly, constructively critical audience and to get valuable feedback.
