December 2-3, 2023 at the University of Waterloo

About

GraphQUON 2023 is the annual pre-SIGGRAPH workshop, organized by Central-Canadian computer graphics labs in Quebec and Ontario. It is an opportunity for students to present their prospective submissions to a friendly, constructively critical audience and to get valuable feedback, as well as an opportunity for initiating cross-institutional collaboration.

Program

The event will be held in person at DC1302 at the University of Waterloo. Streaming is planned for remote participants, but no talks will be recorded.
